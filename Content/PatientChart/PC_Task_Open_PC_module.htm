Open a module

 

This topic describes how to open a module on the .

When the feature starts, you will see the Clinical Summary tab displayed. You can open additional modules to show other patient information.

To open a module on the

  1. Click the Open tab.

  2. Select the module to display from the list presented.

    The modules in the Recommended list are set by the administrator using the Patient Chart Configuration.
