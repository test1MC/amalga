Delete a widget

 

This topic describes how to delete a widget from the Clinical Summary.

Using the Configuration, you can remove a widget from the Clinical Summary.

To delete a widget

  1. Open the Configuration.
  2. Expand Clinical Summary.
  3. Click the name of the widget to be deleted.
  4. Click Delete.
  5. Click OK to confirm the deletion.
