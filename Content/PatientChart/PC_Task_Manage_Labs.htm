Manage the module

 

This topic describes how you can control the display of the module.

results that are out of range are indicated by a red letter (H or L).

To filter the laboratory data displayed

  • Click one of the lab types or click All.

To filter the status

  • Click the drop-down arrow next to Status, select the status or multiple statuses to display, and then click OK.

To view details of a group

  • Click the arrow next to the name to expand or collapse details in the group.

To view a different page

  • Click a page number or click the left or right arrow to scroll through the pages.
