Manage the

 

This topic describes how you can control the display of the .

The consolidates information about the patient that has been retrieved from multiple sources. You can open the modules to view more details about the patient.

The Clinical Summary tab is displayed when you open the Patient Chart. On the Clinical Summary tab, widgets are displayed (for instance, Vital Signs and Problem List).

Widgets are small, limited-functionality versions of the Patient Chart modules. You can open the full module from the widget to view and modify the data.

To open a module on the

  1. Click the Open tab.
  2. Select the module to display from the list presented.

To open a module

  • Click the widget title in the Clinical Summary. The module will be opened on a new tab. Then you will be able to further modify the data displayed by grouping, filtering, or sorting. The functions available depend on the module.

To close a module

  • Click the Close icon () on the tab.

To change the height of a widget

  • Grab the bottom edge of the widget and drag it to the desired size.
