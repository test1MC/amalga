This topic describes the functions you can perform on the page.

From the portal, click Patient Lists.

To sort the lists

To group the lists

  • Click a column heading, and then click Group ascending or Group descending.

To remove a grouping

  • Click a column heading, and then click Remove grouping.

    Grouping a second heading will remove the first grouping. Sorting will not be affected.
