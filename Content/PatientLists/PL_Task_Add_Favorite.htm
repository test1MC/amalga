Add a favorite list

 

This topic describes how to mark a patient list as a favorite.

Favorites give you quick access to the patient lists you use most. Once you mark a patient list as a favorite, it can be viewed on the Favorites tab as well as the All tab.

To mark a patient list as a favorite

  1. Do one of the following:
    1. When browsing lists, click the star next to the name of the list.
    2. When viewing a patient list, click More and then click Add to favorites
