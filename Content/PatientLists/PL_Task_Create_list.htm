Create a patient list

 

This topic describes how to create a patient list by selecting and grouping criteria.

It is possible to create queries that will not produce the results you expect. Before you create a patient list using advanced features such as custom criteria and complex grouping, read this topic carefully. After creating a query, read the criteria from the beginning to the end to determine whether it makes sense. After running the query, examine your results to make sure that they match your expectations.

When you create a patient list, use Quick criteria or Custom criteria and group criteria in complex ways to select patients to add to a patient list.

To create a patient list

  1. From the portal, click .
  2. Click Create a patient list.
  3. In the Create a patient list dialog box, enter a title (required) and a description, and then click OK.
  4. In the dialog box labeled Add patients to your list by defining list criteria, click Set patient list criteria.
  5. In the Patient selection criteria dialog box, do one or both of the following:
    • Expand Quick criteria and click the plus sign (+) next to one or more criteria that describe your patients.
    • Expand Custom criteria and click the plus sign (+) next to one or more criteria that describe your patients.
  6. For each criterion, select an operator and value, and then click OK.
  7. Next to the criterion, select And or Or from the drop-down list.

  8. To rearrange criteria, grab the criterion handle () and drag the criterion to a new location.

    You can group criteria, or groups of criteria, to control the sequence in which they are evaluated.

  9. To group criteria, click the box next to each criterion and click Group selected criteria. To clear the selection, click Ungroup.

    Grouping only works on adjacent criteria. You must have at least three criteria selected for grouping to work.

  10. Select And or Or for each group.
  11. When you have finished adding criteria, click Save.

About criteria

entities represent any healthcare or organizational concept that a hospital or other medical organization might need to manage centrally using . Examples include patients, visits, and lab results.

  • Quick criteria are based on the patient entity.
  • Demographic custom criteria are also based on the patient entity.
  • All other custom criteria are based on other entities (for example, allergy or encounter).
  • An entity group is automatically created when you add a criterion from an entity other than a patient entity. For a patient to match the criteria in an entity group, the patient must have an instance of that entity matching the criteria in the group.
  • Criteria based on only the patient entity are not allowed in entity groups.
  • You can drag a criterion into a group as long as the group is from the same entity.
  • You can drag a criterion out of a group. For non-patient criteria, this action will create a new entity group at the location where the criteria was placed.
