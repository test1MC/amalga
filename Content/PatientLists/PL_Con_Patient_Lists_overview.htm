overview

 

This topic describes the advantages of using .

provides you a way to create meaningful lists of patients who meet specific criteria. The lists can be used to manage patient care issues, handle workflow, and spot trends in hospital admissions. List criteria can be as simple as "all of my patients" or as complex as "all prepubescent children who have not had a visit in the past three years".

Once you have created a list, you can view the list, create and view reports associated with the list, edit list properties, change the patient selection criteria, add or remove columns in the grid view, and share the list with others.
