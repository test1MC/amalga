View a patient list without the pane

 

This topic describes how to view a patient list without the  pane.

When you are finished viewing reports associated with a patient list, you can view the patient list without the pane.

To view a patient list without the pane

  • From the pane, click the View the patient list without the Analytics pane icon ().

    The pane will close and the list will be displayed with all filters cleared. However, if you return to the pane, the filters will still be applied.
