Share a patient list

 

This topic describes how to share a patient list so that it can be viewed by other users.

By default, the author of the patient list has full control over the list, and decides which users can view the list, and what additional rights such users have. The available permission levels are:

  • View only
  • View & edit
  • View, edit, & delete
  • Full control

For more information about what you can do with each of these permission levels, see Roles and permissions on page 1.
