Getting started

Welcome to . This installation reference is designed to guide you through the process of installing and making basic configuration choices for your new environment. Once you have completed the steps in this guide you will have an implementation from which you can build a customized solution for your job site.

It is important that you follow this guide carefully in a step-by-step process. Each part of the installation builds on the next. Skipping steps can create various problems that may lead to parts of the product not being available. Once you have completed the installation, come back to this reference as needed for refreshers on how to repeat certain aspects of the installation as your environment matures.

Who should read this guide
