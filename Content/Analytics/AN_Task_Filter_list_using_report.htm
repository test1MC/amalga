Filter a patient list

 

This topic explains how to use a report to filter a patient list .

Graphical reports can be dynamically filtered by clicking a section or multiple sections that represent the patients you want to see in the patient list.

To filter a list using a report

  1. Click the section of the report or multiple reports that represents the patients you want to see.

    The patient list will be refreshed, showing only the patients that were included in the filtered graph sections. The display will indicate which filters are applied to the list.

    In this example, the first graph is filtered by age group (between 40 and 50 years) and the second graph is filtered by marital status (single) and sex (male). Abbas to provide graphs for screen shot.

  2. To remove the filters, click Clear all.
