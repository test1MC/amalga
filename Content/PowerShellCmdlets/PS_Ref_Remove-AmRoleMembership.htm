Remove-AmRoleMembership

 

The Remove-AmRoleMembership cmdlet removes assignment of a user, group, or computer account from an role.

Amalga built-in role membership required: AmalgaSysAdmin

For more information, see Built-in roles.

Syntax

Remove-AmRoleMembership -RoleName <String> -UserAccount <String>

Parameter descriptions

Example

Add-AmRoleMembership -RoleName ERStaff -UserAccount Contoso\ERDoctors
