Many operational and management tasks can be accomplished by running the cmdlets in this section. The cmdlets are listed alphabetically, and the names are generally descriptive of their intended use.

Many cmdlets that are discoverable within Microsoft.Amalga.Powershell.dll are not intended for customer use. They are included in this section, but their topics contain only a comment about their restricted use.

Prepare to run cmdlets

You can run cmdlets after preparing the environment by using one of the following methods.

To prepare the environment on a computer with the Administration feature installed.

  1. Do one of the following.

    • On the desktop, double-click PowerShell.

    • From the Start menu, open the folder Microsoft Amalga 3.0 and then click PowerShell.

You can now run the cmdlets.
