Get-AmRoleMembership

 

The Get-AmRoleMembership cmdlet lists all user, group, and computer accounts assigned to an role.

Amalga built-in role membership required: AmalgaAppAdmin, AmalgaDataAdmin, AmalgaDataPrivilegedService, AmalgaService, or AmalgaSysAdmin

For more information, see Built-in roles.

Syntax

Get-AmRoleMembership -RoleName <String>

Parameter descriptions

Example

Get-AmRoleMembership -RoleName Nurses
