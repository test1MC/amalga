Clear binding for an entity and its properties

 

This topic describes how to clear binding information for an entity and its properties.

You can clear all binding information for an entity and its properties. When you clear binding information, all binding sources for the entity are removed.


Required Role: AmalgaDataAdmin or AmalgaSysAdmin
