View binding for entity properties

 

This topic describes how to view the binding information for a property of an entity.

You can view the binding information for entity properties.


Required Role: AmalgaAppAdmin, AmalgaDataAdmin, or AmalgaSysAdmin

To view the binding information for a property of an entity

  5. To view details for a property and its bound column, click the property name listed in the Entity Property column.

    A yellow border appears around the selected property and additional details are listed for the property and for its bound column. See Entity binding pane for more information.
