.PatientDemographicHistory entity

 

Patient data can be merged and moved as part of the patient administration process. You might find it useful to search for a patient not just by the latest information, but also by information previously associated with a patient such as an old address or previously used names. The PatientDemographicHistory entity makes some demographic information available for search.

Entity concept code: PD1, PID
