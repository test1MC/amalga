Set your local template folder

This t Topic describes thehas a procedures that is required to use topic templates.

Before you can use topic templates for the first time, you must tell Flare where you want your templates. Use this procedure to do that.

To set your local template folder

  1. In Flare project AmalgaV3, click Tools, then click Manage Templates.
  2. Click the New Template Folder icon.
  3. Browse to C:\UA-SRC\Amalgav3-wking\Content\Standards and Style Guides\Templates\Amalga Templates\Content\. Click the Amalga Templates folder.
  4. Click OK.
  5. Expand the Amalga Templates folder and verify that the template files are there.
