Master pages

 

This topic describes the role of master pages in customizing your sites.

Master pages provide the look and feel and standard behavior that you want for all of the pages in your site. Together with content pages, they produce output that combines the layout of the master page with content from the content page.

supports master pages for defining elements that are common to all pages. You can specify all of the shared elements of your site in the master page or pages, and add page-specific elements to content pages.

Note

Master pages are a feature of ASP.NET. For more information about working with ASP.NET master pages, see ASP.NET Master Pages Overview .

Benefits of master pages

Master pages allow you to make changes in a single location. Here are the benefits:

  • Changes in one page will be reflected in all pages that use the master page.
  • User interface can be reused
  • Pages will be more consistent, providing a better end-user experience

Because all pages associated with a master page have the same look and feel, end users can browse from a core Foundation page to a page added by a third-party solution or to a page that has been customized by the web designer, and not notice a difference in the way the site looks or behaves.

To customize master pages, you must first add them to the list of items in the applicable site template and then deploy them.

In , you deploy master pages to accomplish the following objectives:

  • Customize the site headers, including the organization's logo.
  • Deploy files that are provided with , but are not included by default in the applicable site template.
  • Deploy new files that are not provided with .

When you work with a new file that is not included in the template, you must add it to the template project and then proceed with your customizations.

Location of master pages

There is a master page for each site. Master pages are deployed to this folder: \Program Files\Common Files\Microsoft Shared\Web Server Extensions\14\TEMPLATE\FEATURES\Microsoft.Health.Framework_MasterPagesLayouts.
