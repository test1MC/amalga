Sites

 

This topic describes the sites that are available in the  starter kit.

The following sites will be available in the  starter kit:

  • Internal provider site on page 1 - used by providers inside the institution (nurses, doctors, and others) to view medical information about their patients.
  • Staff site on page 1 - used by institution staff (billing staff, registration staff, and others) to respond to patient and provider inquiries and to manage interactions that happen on the .
  • Patient site on page 1- used by the institution to communicate to patients, family, and caregivers. By default, it uses authentication for logged-in access to patient medical records.
  • Public site - used for an unauthenticated experience for any interested parties (patient, provider, and others).

Accessibility

All portals, end-to-end workflows, and associated controls that are deployed as part of the platform have been through the rigorous Microsoft internal product accessibility lifecycle. By following this process and the extensive set of requirements it contains, the platform conforms to most accessibility standards expected by legal and regulatory bodies around the world, and enables use with most available assistive technologies. The degree to which complies with respect to U.S. regulations is described in the Section 508 VPAT form available on the public Microsoft Accessibility website at http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkID=230880. Follow the Section 508 link to navigate to the VPAT repository for all Microsoft products.

If you add new features to the site, you should plan to achieve the same level of support.
