Configure

 

You must configure settings on the platform logical host.

To configure IIS

  1. From Doors Start, point to Administrative Tools, and click ISS Manager.
  2. Expand ServerName (Domain), expand Sites, expand Default Web Site, and then expandadfs.
  3. Click the ls application and double-click Authentication.
  4. Click Doors Authentication, and click Advanced Settings....
  5. Select Off in the Extended Protection list, and click OK.
