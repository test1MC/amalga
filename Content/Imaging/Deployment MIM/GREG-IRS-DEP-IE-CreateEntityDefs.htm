Create Entity Definitions

 

The following public imaging entities are created as part of the installation along with other entities during the installation of PlatformServices.

  • Imaging.DICOMStudy
  • Imaging.DICOMSeries
  • Imaging.DICOMInstance
  • Imaging.Correlation

The imaging entities are not bound to any databases at this point of the deployment process.
