Verify the deployment of application

 

Verify the deployment of the IDA by following these procedures:

  • Verify the operation of the SOAP service
  • Verify the operation of the REST service

To verify the operation of the SOAP service

  1. From Doors Start, click All Programs, point to , point to Visual Studio Tools, and then click Visual Studio Command Prompt.
  2. Type: WCFTestClient
  3. Right-click My Service Project and then click Add service.
  4. Type https://Your_Machine_FQDN:8801/Your_Instance_Name/Microsoft.Amalga.Imaging.Delivery.SoapService.svc in the Add service box.
  5. Type a studyUUID in the StudyUUID box.
  6. Click Invoke . Verify that an error message is not displayed.

To verify the operation of the REST service

  1. From Doors Start, point to All Programs, click Internet Explorer.
  2. In the address bar, type: https://Your_Machine_FQDN:IDS_RESTful_PortNumber/ImageDeliveryRestService.svc/_
    Retrieve?_
    req=wado&contentType=application_
    %2Fdicom&instanceUUID=abc&securityReference=abc    .
  3. Verify that the browser does not display a HTTP 404 error.
