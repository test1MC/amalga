Interacting with Terminology

 

You can interact with the Terminology platform application using either service contracts or entities. The ITerminologyQueryService service contract provides operations related to retrieving terminology, concept, and relationship information, while the ISemanticLabelTaggingService service contract provides operations related to semantic tag management. The Microsoft.Terminology.Beta entity namespace provides entities related to retrieving terminology, concept, and relationship information that can be bound to application views in the virtual schema of your application.
