Site Definitions

The following sites will be available in the Application Framework starter kit:

  • Internal provider - used by providers inside the institution (nurses, doctors, and others) to view medical record information about their patients.
  • Community provider - used by providers (nurses, doctors, and others) who do not have general access to the institution’s information systems. It is a place where community providers can view medical record information about their patients.
  • Staff - used by institution staff (billing staff, registration staff, and others) to respond to patient and provider inquiries and to manage interactions that happen on the Patient Site and Community Provider Site.
  • Patient - used by the institution to communicate to patients, family, and caregivers. By default, it uses authentication for logged-in access to patient medical record information.
  • Public - used for an unauthenticated experience for any interested parties (patient, community provider, and others)
