Patient Portal Sign In cControl

 

The Patient Portal Sign In control enables patients to sign in to their personal account.

The Patient Portal Sign In control contains a text element with instructions, text boxes for User ID and password, a reset button, and a "Sign in" button to initiate sign in. When the patients enters their credentials and clicks Sign inthe button, they are redirected to the sign in page. When sign in is completed, the user is returned to the pPatient pPortal and the authenticated home page is displayed.

Adding the Patient Portal Sign In control to a page

You can add a Patient Portal Sign In control to a project in by following the instructions in Toolbox controls.

Customizing the Patient Portal Sign In control

You can change the appearance and behavior of each Patient Portal Sign In control instance by editing the control properties in the Properties Window. For more information about properties of the Patient Portal Sign In control, see PatientLinkRequestAccess.
