Patient Portal Sign Up cControl

 

The Patient Portal Sign Up control enables patients to sign up for a account.

The Patient Portal Sign In control contains a text element with instructions, text boxes for User ID and password, a reset button, and a "Sign up" button. When the patients clicks the Sign Up button, they are redirected to , where they can create a new account. Once the sign up process is completed, the user is returned to the pPatient pPortal and the authenticated home page is displayed.

Customizing the Patient Portal Sign Up control

You can change the appearance and behavior of each Patient Portal Sign Up control instance by editing the control properties in the Properties Window. For more information about properties of the Patient Portal SignUp control, see PatientLinkRequestAccess.
