architecture introduction

 

The A architecture was designed with several goals in mind.

  • Make it easy to build, deploy and share applications.
  • Integrate seamlessly with other products.
  • Simplify data access, reporting, dashboards, and unified patient visualization.
  • Reduce the administrative overhead of managing the infrastructure.
