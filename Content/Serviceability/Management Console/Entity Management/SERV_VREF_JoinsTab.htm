 View a list of tables and views that are related to anchor tables in the binding sources for the entity. Table relationships are configured when entity properties are bound to columns from tables or views other than the anchor table. For more information about anchor tables and related tables, see Entity binding overview.

Access For access steps, see Access the tab.
