View physical host information

 

This topic includes instructions for viewing physical host information.

You can view details of the physical hosts in your topology in .

The following image shows the details of the physical host named SHSB02-ENUS-SRV.

To view Platform component information

  1. Open .

    For instructions, see Open the .

  2. In , click System in the module list at the bottom of the navigation pane.
  3. In the navigation pane, expand Logical Hosts, and then expand a logical host.
  4. Click the physical host you want to display.
  5. The physical host information will be displayed in the details pane.
