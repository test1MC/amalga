To synchronize an application

  1. Open .

    For instructions, see Open the .

  2. In , click Application in the module list at the bottom of the navigation pane.
  3. In the navigation pane, click the application to synchronize.
  4. In the task pane on the right, click Sync Application.
  5. In the Sync Application dialog box, select the physical host that you want to synchronize with the application.
  6. Click Sync Selected.
