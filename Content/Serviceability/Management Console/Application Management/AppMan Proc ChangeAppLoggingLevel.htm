Change application service logging levels

 

You can change the diagnostic or performance logging levels of an application's services through the . For information on the available logging levels, see Operational logging.

To change a service logging level through , use the Set-AmConfigurationSetting cmdlet. For instructions on accessing PowerShell cmdlets, see PowerShell reference.


Required Role: AmalgaAppAdmin, or AmalgaSysAdmin.

To change an application service logging level

  3. In the Application Configuration Tasks pane, click Configure Settings. The Configure Application Settings pane will open to display the configurable application and service settings.

  4. Click the service DiagnosticsLogLevel or PerformanceLogLevel setting you wish to configure.

  5. Click the down arrow next to the current setting and select the desired logging setting from the drop-down list.
  6. Click Save.
